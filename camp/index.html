Install This App

To use this app, please install it to your home screen.

Open the browser menu → "Add to Home Screen".

Note: If you already have an older version installed, please remove it first.

After you have installed, close this app, go to your app drawer and long press then drag the new icon to your home screen when it appears.

Help

Actioning a destination button

Single tap → walk details

Long press → opens maps app

Android / Samsung Tip
After launching Google Maps, use your phone’s Back button repeatedly if you want to return to this app. On Samsung devices, the Back button may first navigate through Maps screens before exiting. This is normal — just keep pressing Back until you’re back here.

Google Maps Didn’t Open

Your device didn’t switch to the Google Maps app.

This can happen if:

  • The Maps app isn’t installed
  • Your device opens links in the browser
  • iOS kept the link inside the PWA

You can still continue, but navigation may be limited.
