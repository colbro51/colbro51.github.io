Android / Samsung Tip
After launching Google Maps, use your phone’s Back button repeatedly until you return to this app. On Samsung devices, the Back button may first navigate through Maps screens before exiting. This is normal — just keep pressing Back until you’re back here.

Google Maps Didn’t Open

Your device didn’t switch to the Google Maps app.

This can happen if:

  • The Maps app isn’t installed
  • Your device opens links in the browser
  • iOS kept the link inside the PWA

You can still continue, but navigation may be limited.
