Install Camp on Android

Tap the ⋮ menu button in Chrome

(some browsers use hamburger menu icon)

Then choose Install App

(some devices use Add to home screen)

Exit and launch from new bird icon

Install Camp on iPhone

To use the full app, including Maps, you need to add it to your Home Screen.

  1. Tap the Share button
  2. Choose “Add to Home Screen”
  3. Open the app from the new Camp icon

Tip: If you already installed the app, open it from your Home Screen.
Safari cannot run the full version.

Open Camp

You’ve installed the app before.

Open it from your Home Screen to use Maps and all features.

Safari can only show the install instructions.

Google Maps Didn't Open

Your device didn't switch to the Google Maps app.

This can happen if:

  • The Maps app isn't installed
  • Your device opens links in the browser
  • iOS kept the link inside the PWA

You can still continue, but navigation may be limited.
