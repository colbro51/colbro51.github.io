Hall Location and Parking

How to get there

From Porirua follow Titahi Bay Road for about 3 kilometers. Turn left onto Te Pene Avenue and continue for approximately 1 kilometers. Between the shops and the Fire Station turn right into Niblick Lane. Niblick Hall will be on your left.

To get to the community centre parking area, from Porirua follow Titahi Bay Road for about 3 kilometers and continue along the Main Road to Whitehouse Road on your left. Parking area in 200 metres. Niblick Hall is about 70 metres from the parking area.
